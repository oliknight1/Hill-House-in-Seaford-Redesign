Standard Breakfast

The Full English Breakfast is served between 8 AM - 10 AM, although we are able to cater for an earlier breakfast on request. Our breakfast includes nothing but the finest selection of meat, free range eggs cooked to your preference as well all the usual sides.

Vegetarian Breakfast

The vegetarian options are served between 8AM - 10AM, although we are able to cater for an earlier breakfast on request. We provide a wide selection of fresh fruit and cereals to our customers, as well as offering meat alternatives such as Quorn sausages and bacon.

Cereal

Here at Hill House we provide dinner as well as breakfast for our customers as we want to make life as easy as possible for our residents. Breakfast consists of a choice between cereal, fruit and a classic full English breakfast. Please let the staff know if you have any specific dietary requirements, we will be happy to cater for you.
