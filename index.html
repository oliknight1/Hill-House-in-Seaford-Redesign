Food

Here at Hill House we provide dinner as well as breakfast for our customers as we want to make life as easy as possible for our residents. Breakfast consists of a choice between cereal, fruit and a classic full English breakfast. Please let the staff know if you have any specific dietary requirements, we will be happy to cater for you.

Accomodation

At Hill House we come fully equipped with single, double and family rooms to accommodate all types of stay. All of our rooms come with free WIFI, Freeview TV, fresh clean cotton bedding as well as luxurious Egyptian cotton sheets. Every room has a great view of the beautiful land surrounding the property, feel free to get in contact if you have any questions!
